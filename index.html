Naomi Bredon

I received my PhD at University of Fribourg under the supervision of Ruth Kellerhals. You can find my thesis here. My research interests lie in hyperbolic geometry. I'm working on hyperbolic Coxeter polyhedra in higher dimensions, manifolds, and growth rates.
